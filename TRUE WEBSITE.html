About us

The Problem and Need

• A typical school will use an average of 2,000 sheets per day,
meaning that during a full school year of 160 days, a school will chew through over 320,000 sheets of paper per year.

• With roughly 14,89,115 schools in India, this means that all together schools are consuming as much as 4,76,51,68,00,000 sheets of paper per year, and that is a very very big number.



• In a world where paper waste and environmental concerns are growing, affordability becomes a significant need within Indian communities.

• Many individuals within these communities are actively seeking unique, environmentally conscious yet budget-friendly solutions for their everyday needs.

Our Innovative Solution

• Recycling 1 tonne of paper saves around 1400 liters of oil, 26,500 liters of water and 17 trees.
• Paper accounts for around 26% of landfill waste and 33% of municipal waste.

• Our idea aims to address this pressing need by repurposing old notebook pages into affordable notebooks.

• This offers an innovative, sustainable solution that aligns with the values and budget constraints of Indian minority consumers.

Our Mission and Values

• At our core, we are driven by a dual mission - to reduce paper waste and make quality notebooks accessible to all.

• We believe in the power of repurposing, turning what might have been discarded into valuable tools for learning and creativity.

• Our commitment to sustainability goes beyond just our products. We source materials responsibly, ensuring that every aspect of our business aligns with eco-conscious practices.

•With every purchase, our customers become a part of a movement that not only saves money but also contributes to a more sustainable future for all..
